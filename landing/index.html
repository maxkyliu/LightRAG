Why SmartRAG

Everything you need to ship retrieval.

A production-ready RAG platform — not a notebook. Built for teams, with isolation and cost control from day one.

Graph + vector retrieval

Combines a knowledge graph with semantic search so answers capture relationships, not just nearby text.

Isolated team workspaces

Every team gets its own private workspace. One platform, many tenants — data never leaks across boundaries.

Web & chat access

Query from the SmartRAG web UI or straight from Telegram. Upload, ask, and get sourced answers anywhere.

Built-in resource quotas

Per-team storage and monthly query limits keep costs predictable. Assign a tier and SmartRAG enforces it.

Sourced, traceable answers

Responses point back to the documents they came from, so your team can trust and verify every result.

Deploy in your cloud

Self-host on your own infrastructure with Docker. Your data, your keys, your control — no lock-in.

How it works

From upload to answer in three steps.

1

Upload your documents

Drop in PDFs, text, and files through the web UI or chat. SmartRAG extracts and structures the content for you.

2

It builds the graph

Entities and relationships are extracted into a knowledge graph and indexed for fast semantic retrieval.

3

Ask anything

Query in natural language and get grounded, sourced answers — scoped privately to your team's workspace.
